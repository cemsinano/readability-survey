Research Study

Annual Report Readability Study

University of Ottawa — PhD Research in Management

In this study you will read 50 short excerpts from U.S. corporate annual reports (10-K filings, Management Discussion section) and rate each excerpt on three dimensions of readability. The task takes approximately 60–90 minutes and can be completed across multiple sessions.

What we ask:

  • Read each excerpt carefully before rating.
  • No financial expertise is required. Focus on writing quality, not the business content.
  • Imagine you are a knowledgeable but time-pressed professional reader.
  • Rate each excerpt on three 1–10 scales by moving the sliders.
  • Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. You can close the tab and return later.

Rating scale guide:

1–3  Very difficult / poor 4–6  Moderate 7–10  Easy / excellent
